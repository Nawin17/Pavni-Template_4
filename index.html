Data Archiver for Dataverse

Data Archiver for Dataverse (DDA) is an add-on built for Dynamics 365 that intelligently moves the Emails with their attachments to Microsoft SharePoint for reducing the storage overhead on Dataverse. The primary purpose of the product is to free up the space used by emails over time in D365 to avoid the costly monthly recurring costs for D365 storage. Email activities and attachments account for more than 60% of D365 Database storage.
